ClimOceaNotes

ClimOceaNotes is an online free-access tutorial hub for ocean and climate data analysis. This site will provide comprehensive and detailed step-by-step process on various approaches to analyzing ocean and climate variables such as sea surface temperature, currents, salinity, air temperature, sea-level pressure, winds, etc., using R and Python programming. The topics cover a wide array of applications from introduction to intermediate level of scientific programming.
